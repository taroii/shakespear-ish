Specs + Features

The Project

Great Poetry Taste

Shakespeare[-ish] is a custom trained GPT-2 model that learned from over 35,000 words worth of Shakespearean goodness from a collection of all of his best sonnets.

Interactive

Shakespeare[-ish] will write sonnets based off of any prompt you provide it.

Timeline

Shakespeare[-ish] was completed by some burnt-out 20 year-old over the course of one week!

Potential Improvements

Some potential improvements to this project include expanding it to have different styles from different writers, building a discord bot, or using newer GPT API's to improve its sonnet-writing capabilities!
